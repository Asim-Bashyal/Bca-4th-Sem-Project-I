Our Journey

Our journey began on October, 2024, with the opening of our first multiplex, Student Cinemas - Butwal, located at Butwal Metropolitian City, Rupandehi, Lumbini Province. This flagship location set the standard for comfort, quality, and entertainment that our patrons have come to expect.

Expansion

Now we are expanding our service in Kathmandu from 2025 -StudentCinemas. This facility brought our unique blend of state-of-the-art technology and superior service to the heart of Butwal, quickly becoming a favorite spot for local moviegoers.
